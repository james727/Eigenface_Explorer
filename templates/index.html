EigenFace Explorer

EigenFace Explorer is an interactive web tool that allows you to create the EigenFace decomposition of your face. The application allows you to vary the dimension of the eigenspace your face is projected onto. Why does this exist? I'm not really sure, I thought it'd be fun. Why does anything exist, anyways?
